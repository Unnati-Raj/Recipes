Easy Recipes For Students

As students, we are extremely busy and have very less time to prepare meals. But it is important to have a proper diet for your mind and body to work well. Here are some quick, easy and nutritious recipes to help you stay fit!

Chicken & Veggie Stir-Fry

Cooking Time : 20mins

Feel free to change up the protein or vegetables depending on what you have in your fridge. The simple sauce packs a flavor punch that will bring the dish together, no matter what.

Oven-Baked French Bread Pizzas

Cooking Time : 35mins

Pizza night is the best night — especially when everyone gets to build their dream slice. (Or three!)

Easy Butter Chicken

Cooking Time : 30mins

This simplified version of the Indian classic combines chicken, tomato sauce, and a slew of aromatic spices and herbs — all in one pot, for the simplest cleanup.

Not-your-average Ramen

Cooking Time : 15mins

It takes all of five minutes to fry some bacon + an egg. (Plus some green onions if you're feelin' fancy.)

Meal Prep Pesto Chicken & Veggies

Cooking Time : 35mins

The only thing better than one super easy dinner is being able to make *four* of them at once. This handy meal prep version means you can stash a few away for future Hungry You.

Easiest One-Pot Beef with Broccoli

Cooking Time : 20mins

Chopping the beef into small pieces first means everything cooks through in about five minutes.
